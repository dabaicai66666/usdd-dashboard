收益计算逻辑

Smart Allocator（Aave/Morpho/Spark）
Earning = balance(投资地址) → convert to assets → 价值 + total withdraw - total supply

JustLend / Tron
Earning = jUSDT amount × jUSDT price + total withdraw - total supply

Venus / BSC
Earning = vUSDT amount × rate / 1e36

Spark farm / ETH
Earning = 固化收益 + 浮动收益（SPK token，非复利）

成本计算逻辑

sUSDD 利息（ETH/BSC）

Pie=sUSDD总数, chi=兑换比率
